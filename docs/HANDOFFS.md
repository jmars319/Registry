# tenra Registry Handoffs

tenra Registry stays unique as the rental operating desk. Customers, units, rentals, receivables, imports, and documents are a complete domain.

## Produces

- `tenra-registry.ledger-export.v1` for Ledger receivable import.
- `tenra-registry.assembly-document-request.v1` for Assembly content/document drafting.
- Draft customer copy that can go to Proxy before external delivery.

## Consumes

- Proxy-shaped customer letters, notices, agreement copy, statement copy, and email subjects.
- Guardrail review for externally sent, printed, or money/customer-record-affecting actions.

Registry should not grow bookkeeping, content generation, or policy enforcement beyond its rental domain.
